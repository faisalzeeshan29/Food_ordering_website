Frequently Asked Quetions

How can I cancel my reservation ?

Log in, go to "My Reservations," select the booking, and click "Cancel." Check our policy for details. Need help? Contact support.

How can I change delivery address after order placement ?

To update the delivery address, go to your order details and click "Edit Address." Make the changes and save. If you encounter any issues, contact our support team right away.

How can I contact my delivery person ?

You can contact your delivery person directly through the app. Simply go to your order details and find the option to call or message your driver. If you need further assistance, our support team is also available to help.

My payment is declining. What should I do ?

If your payment is declining, please double-check your payment information for accuracy. Ensure you have sufficient funds in your account. If the issue persists, try an alternate payment method or contact your bank. For further assistance, reach out to our support team.
